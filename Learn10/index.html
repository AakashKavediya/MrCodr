Learning - HTML Video

The video is a combination of audio and visual. HTML videos are of different type. Since it is a combination of audio and visual, it include video type like mp4, wcam, webm, MKV, AVI, MPEG-4, etc. The video can be imported in your website by following ways

  • One of the way is that you can just add a video tag and then you can add a video present on your system.
  • The another way of adding video is that, you can add a < iframe> tag and then you can add a video present on your local system. Or you can add a url of a video from internet or you can add a embed code from youtube.
